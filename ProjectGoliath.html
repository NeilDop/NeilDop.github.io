Project Description

Top down shooter where players face of on a local multiplayer battlefield. Getting kills will award players with level ups, which will increase health and firepower.
It is foretold that the one who spills the most blood on the battlefield will ascend into a GOLIATH, a being powerful enough to plunge the world into chaos.
However, getting to powerful could still shift the favor of the battle against you, as your enemies might unite to take you down.
Who will perish and who will prevail?

Project Details

  • Scripted in C++
  • SFML Framework
  • Self-implemented component based Engine
  • Local multiplayer
  • Controller supported
  • Panning camera

Post Mortem

  • Project Goliath was designed to be a network multiplayer game. Due to time constraints this had to be scrapped.
  • HUD had to be reworked for local multiplayer, this proved to be quite the challenge as there was to much data to be displayed.
  • Component based System worked well for flexibility and expandability.
  • Agile development was messy at first, but once we got used to eachothers workstyle we where on a roll.
  • Eventbus was implemented way to late, leaving older Components tightly coupled.
